The goal of yahtzee is to get the highest score from rolling the dice, of which there are five. The game has 13 rounds and in each round you roll the dice and score in one of 13 categories. you must score once in each category and the score is determinated by each category. The game ends when all categories have been filled. when rolling, you click on the roll dice button to start the round. after the round has started, you have three rerolls to attempt to improve your score. you can lock and unlock the dice by clicking on them. locked dice aren't rerolled. When you score in a category all locked dice are unlocked and you have to click the roll dice button and start a new round to score again. the upper sections scores the sum of all the dice with the mentioned face value. the lower section scores if certainc onditions are met. These are: - three of a kind (have three dice with the same value) score is the sum of the value of all dice - four of a kind (have four dice with the same value) score is the sum of the value of all dice - full house (have three dice of one value and two dice of another value) score is 25 points - small straight (have four dice in sequential order e.g. 1-2-3-4) score is 30 points - large straight (have five dice in sequential order e.g. 1-2-3-4-5) score is 40 points - yahtzee (have five dice with the same value) score is 50 points - chance (no scoring requirements) score is the sum of the value of all dice If the conditions are not met the score in the category will be zero. You have to score in each round and each category can only be scored once
